## Why do you need your own domain?

A domain is the address people type in to find your website — for example **mycompany.no**. Your own domain gives your business a professional look and makes it easy for customers to find you.

<Info>
  **You can now buy your domain directly in Nettify.** Everything is under **Domain** in your dashboard — no external provider and no technical setup.
</Info>

## How to buy a domain in Nettify

<Steps>
  <Step title="Go to Domain in your dashboard">
    Log in at [nettify.no](https://nettify.no) and open **Domain** in your dashboard.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Search for the domain you want">
    Type in your desired domain name, for example "mycompany.no". You'll immediately see if it's available.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Choose your domain">
    Found an available domain you like? Select it and continue to checkout.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Complete your purchase">
    Pay 99 kr for the year — or get the domain free if you're on the Nettsidebygger plan. Nettify handles the registration for you.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Done — your domain is connected automatically">
    Domains you buy in Nettify are connected to your website automatically. You don't need to do any technical setup yourself.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Tips for choosing a domain name

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Keep it short and simple">
    A short domain name is easier to remember and type. **mycompany.no** is better than **my-company-services-and-consulting.no**.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Use your business name">
    The most natural choice is to use your company name. That way customers find you easily.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Avoid hyphens if possible">
    Domains without hyphens are easier to remember and communicate verbally.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Choose .no for the Norwegian market">
    Norwegian customers trust .no domains. It signals that you're a Norwegian business.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

<Warning>
  **Don't wait too long!** Popular domain names get taken quickly. If you find a good name that's available, register it right away.
</Warning>
