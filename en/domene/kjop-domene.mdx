## Why do you need your own domain?

A domain is the address people type in to find your website — for example **mycompany.com**. Your own domain gives your business a professional look and makes it easy for customers to find you.

<Info>
  Nettify doesn't sell domains directly, but we recommend domain providers that work seamlessly with our platform.
</Info>

## How to buy a domain

<Steps>
  <Step title="Choose a provider">
    Go to [One.com](https://www.one.com). We recommend One.com for buying .no domains.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Search for the domain you want">
    Type in your desired domain name, for example "mycompany.com". You'll immediately see if it's available.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Choose a domain extension">
    For local businesses we recommend your country's extension (e.g. **.no** for Norway, **.co.uk** for the UK). You can also consider **.com** for an international audience.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Register and pay">
    Follow the provider's registration process. You usually just need your name, email and payment information.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Connect the domain to Nettify">
    Once the domain is purchased, you're ready to connect it to your website in Nettify.

    <Card title="Next step: Connect your domain" icon="link" href="/en/domene/koble-til-domene">
      Follow our simple guide to connect your domain to Nettify.
    </Card>
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Tips for choosing a domain name

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Keep it short and simple">
    A short domain name is easier to remember and type. **mycompany.com** is better than **my-company-services-and-consulting.com**.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Use your business name">
    The most natural choice is to use your company name. That way customers find you easily.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Avoid hyphens if possible">
    Domains without hyphens are easier to remember and communicate verbally.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Choose the right extension">
    Local customers trust local domain extensions. It signals that you're a local business.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

<Warning>
  **Don't wait too long!** Popular domain names get taken quickly. If you find a good name that's available, register it right away.
</Warning>
