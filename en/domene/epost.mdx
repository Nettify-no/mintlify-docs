## Professional email on your domain

An email address on your own domain — for example **hello@mycompany.no** — looks more professional than a free Gmail or Hotmail address. It builds trust with customers and strengthens your brand.

<Info>
  **Nettify doesn't have its own webmail yet.** That's why we recommend **Google Workspace** for email on your own domain. It's secure, easy to use and works seamlessly with the domain you have in Nettify.
</Info>

## How to set up email

<Steps>
  <Step title="Sign up for Google Workspace">
    Go to [Google Workspace](https://workspace.google.com/solutions/business-email/) and create an account for your business. Choose your domain when prompted.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Copy the MX record">
    After signing up, Google shows you an **MX record**. It tells the internet where email for your domain should be delivered. Copy the value.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Add the MX record in Nettify">
    Go to **Domain** in your Nettify dashboard and paste in the MX record you got from Google. This tells your domain to send email to Google Workspace.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Follow the final steps at Google">
    Go back to Google Workspace and complete the setup. Google automatically checks that the MX record is added correctly.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Done — send and receive email">
    You can now send and receive email on your own domain. It may take a little while before everything is active.
  </Step>
</Steps>
