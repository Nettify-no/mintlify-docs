## How to edit text

Editing text on your website is the easiest thing you can do in Nettify. Just tell Nettify AI what you want to change and what the new text should be.

---

## Ready-made prompts for common text changes

### Change a heading

<Tip>
  **Copy this prompt and paste it into Nettify AI:**
</Tip>

```
Change the main heading on the homepage to "[your new heading]".
Keep the same font size and style.
```

---

### Write a new welcome text

<Tip>
  **Copy this prompt and paste it into Nettify AI:**
</Tip>

```
Write a new welcome text for our homepage. We are [company name], and we work with [industry/services] in [location].
The text should:
- Be warm and inviting, yet professional
- Mention our key services: [service 1], [service 2], [service 3]
- Be a maximum of 2-3 sentences
- End with a call to action, such as "Get in touch for a free consultation."
```

---

### Update contact info

<Tip>
  **Copy this prompt and paste it into Nettify AI:**
</Tip>

```
Update the contact information on the website with the following:
- Phone: [your number]
- Email: [your email]
- Address: [your address]
- Opening hours: Monday-Friday 08:00-16:00, Saturday 10:00-14:00, Sunday Closed
Update this everywhere contact info appears — in the footer, contact section and possibly in the menu.
```

---

### Change button text

<Tip>
  **Copy this prompt and paste it into Nettify AI:**
</Tip>

```
Change all button text on the homepage:
- "Submit" -> "Send message"
- "Read more" -> "Learn more"
- "Contact" -> "Get in touch with us"
- "Learn more" -> "See details"
Make sure all buttons have clear, action-oriented text.
```

---

### Write a new "About us" text

<Tip>
  **Copy this prompt and paste it into Nettify AI:**
</Tip>

```
Write a new "About us" text for our website. Here is the information:
- Company name: [name]
- Founded: [year]
- Industry: [industry]
- Number of employees: [number]
- Location: [location]
- What makes us unique: [describe briefly]

The text should:
- Be 3-4 paragraphs
- Have a personal and trustworthy tone
- Start with a short story about how the company began
- Mention our values and what we care about
- End with why customers should choose us
```

---

### Add opening hours

<Tip>
  **Copy this prompt and paste it into Nettify AI:**
</Tip>

```
Add opening hours to the contact section and footer:
- Monday - Friday: 08:00 - 16:00
- Saturday: 10:00 - 14:00
- Sunday: Closed
Display them as a neat, compact list with a clock icon.
```

## Let Nettify AI write for you

You don't always need to know exactly what the text should say. Nettify AI can help you formulate the content.

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Ask for a new wording">
    "Rewrite our welcome text so it sounds more inviting"
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Give direction without writing everything yourself">
    "Write a short description of our services. We do painting, wallpapering and flooring in Austin"
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Adjust the tone">
    "Make the text more informal and friendly" or "Rewrite the text so it sounds more professional"
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

<Info>
  You can change text on all pages, not just the homepage. Just tell Nettify AI which page you want to work on.
</Info>
