## How to edit text

Editing text on your website is the easiest thing you can do in Nettify. Just tell Nettify AI what you want to change and what the new text should be.

---

## Change a specific text

<Steps>
  <Step title="Point to the text you want to change">
    Tell Nettify AI which text you want to replace. You can refer to the location, the text itself, or both.

    <Tip>
      **Example:** "Change the heading 'Welcome' to 'Welcome to Hansen Construction Ltd'"
    </Tip>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Provide the new text">
    Write the new text, or ask Nettify AI to write it for you.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Review the change">
    Check that the text is correct. Adjust if needed.
  </Step>
</Steps>

---

## Let Nettify AI write for you

You don't always need to know exactly what the text should say. Nettify AI can help you formulate the content.

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Ask for a new wording">
    "Rewrite our welcome text so it sounds more inviting"
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Give direction without writing everything yourself">
    "Write a short description of our services. We do painting, wallpapering and flooring in Trondheim"
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Adjust the tone">
    "Make the text more informal and friendly" or "Rewrite the text so it sounds more professional"
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Translate or adapt">
    "Add a Norwegian version of the welcome text" or "Make the text shorter and more direct"
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

---

## Example instructions

```
"Change the heading to 'Your local plumber since 1995'"
```

```
"Update the phone number in the contact section to 22 33 44 55"
```

```
"Write a new 'About us' text. We are three founders who started the company in 2020. We are passionate about sustainable construction."
```

```
"Change the button text from 'Send' to 'Get in touch with us'"
```

```
"Add opening hours at the bottom of the page: Mon-Fri 08-16, Sat 10-14"
```

<Info>
  You can change text on all pages, not just the homepage. Just tell Nettify AI which page you want to work on.
</Info>
