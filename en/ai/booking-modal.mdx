## What is a booking popup?

A booking popup (also called a modal) is a window that appears when a customer clicks a button — for example **"Book appointment"** or **"Schedule a meeting"**. Instead of sending the customer to another page, the booking form opens right on your website. This creates a smoother experience and more bookings.

---

## How to create a booking popup

<Steps>
  <Step title="Describe what the popup should contain">
    Tell Nettify AI what kind of booking you need and which fields the customer should fill out.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Choose where the button should go">
    Tell Nettify AI where on the website the **"Book appointment"** button should appear — for example in the hero section, on the services page, or in the menu.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Customize the look">
    Ask Nettify AI to adjust colors, text or fields to match your business.
  </Step>
</Steps>

---

## Example instructions

```
"Create a booking popup that opens when you click 'Book appointment'.
The form should have fields for: Name, Phone, Email, Preferred date, and Message.
Place the button in the hero section."
```

```
"Create a booking modal for our hair salon.
The customer should choose between: Men's haircut, Women's haircut, or Coloring.
Add fields for name, phone and preferred time."
```

```
"Add a 'Book a consultation' button on the services page.
When clicked, a popup opens with a short form:
Name, Address, Phone, and a description of the job."
```

## Tips for a good booking popup

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Keep it short" icon="list-check">
    Don't ask for more information than necessary. Name, phone and preferred time is often enough to get started.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Clear button text" icon="hand-pointer">
    Use specific text like **"Book appointment"**, **"Schedule a visit"**, or **"Get a quote"** — avoid generic buttons like "Submit".
  </Card>
  <Card title="Confirmation message" icon="circle-check">
    Ask Nettify AI to add a confirmation after submission, like "Thank you! We'll contact you within 24 hours."
  </Card>
  <Card title="Mobile friendly" icon="mobile-screen">
    Nettify AI automatically creates popups that work well on both mobile and desktop.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

<Info>
  **Using an external booking system?** You can also embed Calendly, Tidycal or similar in a popup. See the guide for [HTML widgets](/en/ai/html-widget).
</Info>

## Where do the bookings go?

All bookings submitted through the popup land under the **"Inquiries"** tab at the bottom of your Nettify dashboard. Here you get an overview of all submitted forms with name, contact info and message.

<Tip>
  **Have Nettify PRO?** You'll also receive email notifications each time someone submits a booking — so you never miss an inquiry.
</Tip>
