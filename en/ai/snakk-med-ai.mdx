## How to talk to Nettify AI

Nettify AI works as a digital assistant for your website. You write what you want in plain language, and Nettify AI makes the changes for you. No coding or technical terms needed.

### Write clear instructions

The more specific you are, the better the result. Think of it as giving instructions to a colleague.

## Examples of what you can say to Nettify AI

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Change text and content">
    - "Change the heading to 'We build your dream home'"
    - "Rewrite the 'About us' text to mention that we have 15 years of experience"
    - "Add our phone number to the contact info: 987 65 432"
    - "Replace the email address with post@mycompany.com"
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Change design and appearance">
    - "Make the headings darker"
    - "Use warmer colors on the website"
    - "Make the text a bit larger"
    - "Add more space between the sections"
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Add new content">
    - "Create a new section with customer testimonials"
    - "Add a price list for our services"
    - "Create a 'Frequently asked questions' section"
    - "Add a map showing where we're located"
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Change structure">
    - "Move the contact section to the bottom of the page"
    - "Create a separate page for the price list"
    - "Remove the partners section"
    - "Add a new subpage for 'Our services'"
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Tips for best results

<Steps>
  <Step title="Do one thing at a time">
    It's better to give one clear instruction at a time than to ask for many changes at once. That way you see the result of each change and can adjust as you go.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Be specific about location">
    If you want to change something specific, tell Nettify AI where on the page the change should happen. For example: "Change the text **in the contact section**" or "Add an image **at the top of the homepage**".
  </Step>

  <Step title="Give examples">
    If you know roughly what you want, give an example. "Write a welcome text in the style of: 'Welcome! We've been helping customers since 2010.'"
  </Step>

  <Step title="Give feedback if it's not right">
    Not quite what you wanted? Just say what should change. "The heading is too long, make it shorter" or "Use a different wording, this sounds too formal".
  </Step>
</Steps>
