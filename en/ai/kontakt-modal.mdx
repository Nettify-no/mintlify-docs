## What is a contact popup?

A contact popup is a window that opens when a customer clicks **"Contact us"** or a similar button. Instead of scrolling to the bottom of the page or going to a separate contact page, the customer gets a form right away. Quick and easy — for both you and the customer.

---

## How to create a contact popup

<Steps>
  <Step title="Describe the form you need">
    Tell Nettify AI which fields you want in the contact form.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Choose where the button goes">
    Tell Nettify AI where the **"Contact us"** button should be — in the navigation, in a section, or as a floating button in the corner of the page.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Customize design and text">
    Adjust the heading, button text, colors and confirmation message to your liking.
  </Step>
</Steps>

---

## Example instructions

```
"Create a contact popup that opens from the 'Contact us' button in the menu.
Fields: Name, Email, Phone and Message.
Show a confirmation: 'Thank you for your inquiry! We'll respond within 24 hours.'"
```

```
"Add a floating 'Get in touch' button in the bottom right corner on all pages.
When clicked, a popup opens with a short contact form."
```

```
"Create a 'Get a free quote' popup.
Fields: Company name, Contact person, Email, Phone and Description of needs.
Place the button on the services page."
```

## Choose the right type of contact form

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Simple contact" icon="envelope">
    Name, email and message. Perfect for general inquiries.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Quote request" icon="file-invoice">
    Add fields for service type, budget or project description.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Feedback" icon="message-smile">
    Short form for collecting customer feedback.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Support" icon="headset">
    Include order number or subject to sort inquiries.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

<Tip>
  **Combine with booking!** You can have both a contact popup and a [booking popup](/en/ai/booking-modal) on the same page — with different buttons.
</Tip>

---

## Where do the inquiries go?

All messages submitted through the contact popup land under the **"Inquiries"** tab at the bottom of your Nettify dashboard. Here you see all submitted forms collected in one place.

<Tip>
  **Have Nettify PRO?** You'll also receive email notifications each time someone submits an inquiry — so you can respond quickly.
</Tip>
