## What is a contact popup?

A contact popup is a window that opens when a customer clicks **"Contact us"** or a similar button. Instead of scrolling to the bottom of the page or going to a separate contact page, the customer gets a form right away. Quick and easy — for both you and the customer.

---

## Ready-made prompts for contact popups

### Simple contact popup

<Tip>
  **Copy this prompt and paste it into Nettify AI:**
</Tip>

```
Create a contact popup with a clean and modern design that opens from the "Contact us" button in the menu.

The popup should have:
- Heading: "Send Us a Message"
- Short text: "We respond to all inquiries within 24 hours."
- Form fields with nice design:
  - Name (required)
  - Email (required)
  - Phone (optional)
  - Message (large text field, required)
- "Send message" button in the accent color with hover effect
- Close button (X) in the top right corner

Design: white modal with rounded corners, dark overlay background, subtle shadow. Form fields should have clean borders, good labels and focus effect. Smooth animation.

Confirmation after submission: "Thank you for your inquiry! We'll get back to you as soon as possible."
```

### Quote request

<Tip>
  **Copy this prompt and paste it into Nettify AI:**
</Tip>

```
Create a "Get a Free Quote" popup with a professional design.

The popup should have:
- Heading: "Get a Free Quote"
- Short text: "Tell us about your project and we'll send you a quote."
- Form fields:
  - Company name (optional)
  - Contact person (required)
  - Email (required)
  - Phone (required)
  - Type of service (dropdown): "[Service 1]", "[Service 2]", "[Service 3]", "Other"
  - Budget (dropdown): "Under $1,000", "$1,000 - $5,000", "Over $5,000", "Not sure"
  - Description of needs (text field)
- "Request quote" button
- Close button

Design: clean and trust-building. Good spacing between fields. Labels above each field.

Confirmation: "Thank you! We'll send you a quote within 2 business days."

Place the "Get a quote" button on the homepage, services page and in the menu.
```

### Floating contact button

<Tip>
  **Copy this prompt and paste it into Nettify AI:**
</Tip>

```
Add a floating "Get in touch" button in the bottom right corner on all pages with a nice design.

The button should:
- Be round with a message icon
- Have the accent color with hover effect
- Always be visible when scrolling
- Not block important content on mobile

When clicked, a popup opens with:
- Heading: "How can we help?"
- Form fields: Name, Email, Message
- "Send" button
- Close button

Design: modern chat bubble style. Smooth animation on open.

Confirmation: "Received! We'll be in touch soon."
```

## Where do the inquiries go?

All messages submitted through the contact popup land under the **"Inquiries"** tab at the bottom of your Nettify dashboard. Here you see all submitted forms collected in one place.

<Tip>
  **Have Nettify PRO?** You'll also receive email notifications each time someone submits an inquiry — so you can respond quickly.
</Tip>

<Tip>
  **Combine with booking!** You can have both a contact popup and a [booking popup](/en/ai/booking-modal) on the same page — with different buttons.
</Tip>
