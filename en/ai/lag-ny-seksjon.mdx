## What is a section?

A section is a separate part of your website. Think of it as building blocks — each section has its own content and purpose. For example, your homepage might have these sections:

- **Hero** — the top part with your main message
- **Services** — an overview of what you offer
- **About us** — a brief introduction to the company
- **Testimonials** — what your customers say about you
- **Contact** — contact information and form

---

## How to create a new section

Tell Nettify AI what kind of section you want and what it should contain.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open your website in Nettify">
    Log in and select the website you want to edit.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Describe the section you want">
    Tell Nettify AI what you're looking for. Be specific about the content.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Review the result">
    Nettify AI creates the section for you. Review the content and design.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Adjust if needed">
    Not entirely happy? Ask Nettify AI to adjust.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Popular section types with ready-made prompts

### Services / Products

Your website should make it crystal clear what you offer. A services section gives visitors a quick overview of what you do, so they can see right away if you are the right fit. It is often the first thing potential customers look for.

<Tip>
  **Copy this prompt and paste it into Nettify AI:**
</Tip>

```
Create a services section on the homepage. It should have:
- A heading: "Our Services"
- A short intro: "We offer a wide range of services tailored to your needs."
- 3 service cards side by side, each with:
  - A relevant icon
  - Service name (e.g. "Consulting", "Installation", "Maintenance")
  - A short description of 1-2 sentences
  - A "Learn more" button
- Design: clean and modern. Cards should have light shadow and rounded corners.
```

### Customer testimonials

People trust other customers more than they trust ads. Adding real testimonials to your website shows visitors that others have had a great experience with you, which makes new customers feel comfortable choosing your business.

<Tip>
  **Copy this prompt and paste it into Nettify AI:**
</Tip>

```
Create a testimonials section on the homepage. It should have:
- A heading: "What our customers say"
- 3 testimonial cards with:
  - Quote in quotation marks
  - Customer name and optional company
  - Star rating (5 stars)
  - A small profile picture placeholder (circle)
- Design: carousel or grid layout. Use quotation marks as a visual element. Light background with subtle shadows.

Use these testimonials:
- "Fantastic service and fast delivery!" — Jane Smith, Brighton
- "Professional from start to finish. Highly recommended!" — John Davis, London
- "We are very pleased with the result." — Company Ltd, Manchester
```

### FAQ (Frequently Asked Questions)

Tired of answering the same questions over and over? An FAQ section handles that for you, giving customers quick answers and saving you time. It also makes your business look well-organized and transparent.

<Tip>
  **Copy this prompt and paste it into Nettify AI:**
</Tip>

```
Create a FAQ section on the homepage, just above the contact section. It should have:
- A heading: "Frequently Asked Questions"
- A short intro: "Find answers to our most common questions."
- Questions as expandable elements (accordion) where you click to see the answer:
  1. "How much do your services cost?" — "Prices vary depending on the scope. Contact us for a free quote."
  2. "What is the delivery time?" — "We normally deliver within 5-10 business days."
  3. "Do you offer a warranty?" — "Yes, we provide a 2-year warranty on all work."
  4. "Do you serve my area?" — "We mainly operate in [area], but get in touch to see if we can help you."
- Design: clean and clear. Each question should have a plus/minus icon.
```

### Price list

Being upfront about your prices builds trust and helps customers decide faster. A clear price list on your website means fewer back-and-forth messages and more people ready to buy when they get in touch.

<Tip>
  **Copy this prompt and paste it into Nettify AI:**
</Tip>

```
Create a pricing section. It should have:
- A heading: "Our Pricing"
- A short intro: "Choose the plan that works best for you."
- 3 pricing cards side by side:
  1. "Basic" — $29/mo — Includes: "Core service", "Email support", "Monthly report"
  2. "Plus" — $59/mo — Includes: Everything in Basic + "Priority support", "Weekly report", "Consulting"
  3. "Premium" — $99/mo — Includes: Everything in Plus + "Dedicated contact person", "Daily follow-up", "Custom solutions"
- The middle card (Plus) should be highlighted as "Most popular"
- Each card should have a "Choose plan" button
- Design: professional with clear visual hierarchy. Premium can have a different background color.
```

### Team / About us

People like doing business with people, not faceless companies. Introducing your team helps customers feel a personal connection to your business and builds the kind of trust that turns visitors into loyal customers.

<Tip>
  **Copy this prompt and paste it into Nettify AI:**
</Tip>

```
Create a "Meet the team" section on the About us page. It should have:
- A heading: "The people behind [company name]"
- A short intro: "We're a small, dedicated team with big ambitions."
- Team cards for each person with:
  - Round profile picture (placeholder)
  - Name
  - Job title
  - A short bio of 1-2 sentences
  - Link to LinkedIn (icon)

Team members:
- "Jane Smith" — CEO — "Founder with 15 years of industry experience."
- "John Davis" — Project Manager — "Keeps all our projects on track with a smile."
- "Sarah Johnson" — Technical Lead — "Our expert on all things technical."
```

### Contact form

Make it as easy as possible for customers to reach you. A contact form right on your website means people can send you a message any time, even outside business hours. More inquiries means more potential sales.

<Tip>
  **Copy this prompt and paste it into Nettify AI:**
</Tip>

```
Create a contact section at the bottom of the homepage. It should have:
- A heading: "Get in touch"
- A short intro: "We respond to all inquiries within 24 hours."
- Two columns:
  - Left: Contact info with an icon for each:
    - Phone: +44 123 456 7890
    - Email: hello@company.com
    - Address: 1 Main Street, London
    - Hours: Mon-Fri 08-16
  - Right: A contact form with fields for Name, Email, Phone, Message and a "Send message" button
- Design: dark background with light text for contrast. Form fields should be clean with rounded corners.
```

### Map and address

Help customers find you without any hassle. An embedded map on your website makes it easy for people to get directions and see exactly where you are located. Perfect for any business with a physical location that customers visit.

<Tip>
  **Copy this prompt and paste it into Nettify AI:**
</Tip>

```
Create a map section in the contact area. It should have:
- A heading: "Find us"
- Two columns:
  - Left: An embedded Google Maps map showing our address: [your address]
  - Right: Contact information with address, phone, email and a "Get directions" button
- Design: the map should take about 60% of the width. Clean layout with good contrast.
```
