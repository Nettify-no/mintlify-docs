## How to change images

You can easily replace images on your website by talking to Nettify AI. You can either upload your own images or ask Nettify AI to find suitable ones for you.

---

## Upload your own images

Using real photos of your business, team, or products makes your website feel genuine and helps customers connect with you. People trust businesses more when they can see who they are dealing with.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Choose the image you want to replace">
    Tell Nettify AI which image you want to change. Be clear about where it is.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Upload the new image">
    Upload the image you want to use. Nettify AI places it for you.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Review the result">
    Check that the image looks good. If you want to adjust the size or placement, just tell Nettify AI.
  </Step>
</Steps>

Got a great photo you want on your site? Just upload it and tell Nettify AI where to put it. Your own images make your website stand out and look more trustworthy than generic photos.

<Tip>
  **Copy this prompt and paste it into Nettify AI:**
</Tip>

```
Replace the main image in the hero section on the homepage with the image I'm uploading now.
Make sure the image covers the full width of the section.
Keep the dark overlay so the text remains readable.
```

---

## Let Nettify AI find images

Do not have your own photos yet? No worries. Nettify AI can pick out professional-looking images that match your business, so your website looks polished and complete right away.

<Tip>
  **Copy this prompt and paste it into Nettify AI:**
</Tip>

```
Find a professional image that fits our services section.
We work in [your industry]. The image should show [describe what you want to see, e.g. "a craftsman at work in a modern home"].
Use warm, bright colors. The image should feel authentic and professional — not generic.
Place it as a background image in the services section.
```

## Ready-made prompts for common image changes

### Replace hero image

The hero image is the first thing visitors see when they land on your site. A fresh, high-quality image here sets the tone for your entire business and makes a strong first impression.

<Tip>
  **Copy this prompt and paste it into Nettify AI:**
</Tip>

```
Replace the background image in the hero section on the homepage.
Find an image that shows [describe industry/theme, e.g. "a modern office with natural light"].
The image should have a dark overlay (50% opacity) so the white text is easy to read.
Make sure the image is high resolution and in landscape format.
```

---

### Add images to service cards

Adding images to your services makes them feel more real and helps customers quickly understand what you offer. Visual details like this make your website look more professional and keep visitors interested.

<Tip>
  **Copy this prompt and paste it into Nettify AI:**
</Tip>

```
Add images to each service card in the services section:
- Card 1 "[service 1]": Find an image that shows [description]
- Card 2 "[service 2]": Find an image that shows [description]
- Card 3 "[service 3]": Find an image that shows [description]
The images should be the same size and style. Use rounded corners.
```

---

### Update the image gallery

Keep your gallery fresh with your latest work or products. An up-to-date gallery shows customers that your business is active and thriving, and gives them more reasons to choose you.

<Tip>
  **Copy this prompt and paste it into Nettify AI:**
</Tip>

```
Update the image gallery on the projects page with the images I'm uploading now.
Replace all existing images with the new ones.
Display them in a 3-column grid with equal sizes.
Add a hover effect that shows the project name over the image.
```

## Tips for good images

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Use your own images when you can">
    Photos of your own team, premises or products build more trust than generic stock photos. Customers want to see who they're dealing with.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Choose images with good quality">
    Blurry or dark images give an unprofessional impression. Use images with good lighting and resolution.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Think about what the image communicates">
    The image should support your message. Selling renovation services? Show completed projects. Running a restaurant? Show food and ambiance.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Remember to take landscape photos">
    Images in landscape format work best on websites. They fill the space better and look more professional.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
