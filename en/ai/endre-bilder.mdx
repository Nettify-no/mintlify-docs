## How to change images

You can easily replace images on your website by talking to Nettify AI. You can either upload your own images or ask Nettify AI to find suitable ones for you.

---

## Upload your own images

Do you have your own photos of the business, products or team? Perfect! Your own images make the website more personal and trustworthy.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Choose the image you want to replace">
    Tell Nettify AI which image you want to change. Be clear about where it is.

    <Tip>
      **Example:** "Replace the image at the top of the homepage" or "Change the background image in the hero section".
    </Tip>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Upload the new image">
    Upload the image you want to use. Nettify AI places it for you.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Review the result">
    Check that the image looks good. If you want to adjust the size or placement, just tell Nettify AI.
  </Step>
</Steps>

---

## Let Nettify AI find images

Don't have your own images? No problem. Nettify AI can find professional images that match your content.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Describe what you want">
    Tell Nettify AI what kind of image you're looking for.

    <Tip>
      **Example:** "Find an image of a cozy cafe with warm lighting" or "Use an image of a craftsman at work".
    </Tip>
  </Step>

  <Step title="See the suggestion">
    Nettify AI finds an image and shows it to you.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Approve or request a new one">
    Like it? Great! If not, ask for another: "Try a different image, preferably with brighter colors".
  </Step>
</Steps>

---

## Tips for good images

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Use your own images when you can">
    Photos of your own team, premises or products build more trust than generic stock photos. Customers want to see who they're dealing with.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Choose images with good quality">
    Blurry or dark images give an unprofessional impression. Use images with good lighting and resolution. Most modern phones take photos that are more than good enough.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Think about what the image communicates">
    The image should support your message. Selling renovation services? Show completed projects. Running a restaurant? Show food and ambiance.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Remember to take landscape photos">
    Images in landscape format (wider than they are tall) work best on websites. They fill the space better and look more professional.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
