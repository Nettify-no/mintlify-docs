## When do you need a new page?

Most websites have more than just a homepage. With Nettify you can easily create new subpages to organize your content.

---

## How to create a new page

<Steps>
  <Step title="Tell Nettify AI what the page should be about">
    Give a clear description of what the page should contain and who it's for.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Nettify AI builds the page for you">
    Nettify AI creates the page with content, layout and design that matches the rest of your website.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Review and adjust">
    Go through the page. Want to change something? Just say so.
  </Step>

  <Step title="The page is automatically added to the menu">
    The new page appears in your website's navigation.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Ready-made prompts for common pages

### Services page

A dedicated services page lets you go into detail about everything you offer. When customers can clearly see what you do and how you can help them, they are much more likely to get in touch.

<Tip>
  **Copy this prompt and paste it into Nettify AI:**
</Tip>

```
Create a new page called "Our Services". It should have:

1. Hero section:
   - Heading: "Services We Offer"
   - Short intro: "We help you with everything from [service 1] to [service 3]."
   - A nice background image that fits our industry

2. Services section with 3-4 services in a clean grid, each with:
   - Icon or image
   - Service name
   - Description of 2-3 sentences
   - "Contact us" button

3. "How We Work" section with 3-4 numbered steps with icons and lines between them

4. CTA section at the bottom: "Ready to get started?" with a contact button

Design: professional, clean and modern with good spacing between sections.
```

### About us page

This is where you tell your story. An About us page helps customers get to know the people behind the business, which builds trust and sets you apart from competitors who feel impersonal.

<Tip>
  **Copy this prompt and paste it into Nettify AI:**
</Tip>

```
Create an "About Us" page. Here is the information:

Company name: [name]
Founded: [year]
Location: [location]
Number of employees: [number]
Industry: [industry]
About us: [describe the company in a few sentences]

The page should have a great and professional design with:
1. Hero with heading "About [company name]" and an inspiring background image
2. An "Our Story" section with 2-3 paragraphs and an image beside the text
3. An "Our Values" section with 3 values as icons with short text in a clean grid
4. A "Meet the Team" section with profile cards (round image, name, title, short bio)
5. A CTA at the bottom: "Want to learn more? Get in touch!" with a nice button

Design: good spacing, modern typography and subtle animations.
```

### Contact page

A proper contact page with a form, phone number, and map makes it easy for customers to reach you however they prefer. The easier it is to get in touch, the more inquiries you will receive.

<Tip>
  **Copy this prompt and paste it into Nettify AI:**
</Tip>

```
Create a contact page with a clean and inviting design. It should have:

1. Heading: "Contact Us"
2. Short intro: "We're here to help. Send us a message, give us a call, or stop by."
3. Two columns with a nice layout:
   - Left: Contact form with fields for Name, Email, Phone, Subject (dropdown), Message and a "Send message" button with hover effect
   - Right: Contact info with icons for phone, email, address and opening hours. Nicely designed with good spacing.
4. Full-width Google Maps map below the columns showing our address: [your address]
5. FAQ section with 3-4 common questions about contact and response time

Contact info:
- Phone: [number]
- Email: [email]
- Address: [address]
- Opening hours: Mon-Fri 08-16

Design: clean, modern, with rounded corners on form fields and subtle shadows.
```

### Pricing page

Customers appreciate knowing what things cost before they reach out. A clear pricing page builds trust, saves you time on back-and-forth, and helps attract customers who are ready to buy.

<Tip>
  **Copy this prompt and paste it into Nettify AI:**
</Tip>

```
Create a pricing page with a professional and clear design. It should have:

1. Heading: "Pricing"
2. Short intro: "Transparent pricing with no hidden costs."
3. Pricing cards in a clean grid with visual hierarchy:
   - [Service 1]: [price] — includes [what's included]
   - [Service 2]: [price] — includes [what's included]
   - [Service 3]: [price] — includes [what's included]
   The middle card should be highlighted as "Most Popular" with an accent color
4. Each card should have a checklist of what's included and a "Choose plan" button
5. A FAQ section with questions about pricing
6. CTA at the bottom: "Not sure what you need? Contact us for a custom quote."

Design: modern pricing table layout with rounded corners, subtle shadows and good typography.
```

### FAQ page

A FAQ page answers your customers' most common questions before they even have to ask. This saves you time, makes your business look professional, and helps visitors feel confident about choosing you.

<Tip>
  **Copy this prompt and paste it into Nettify AI:**
</Tip>

```
Create a FAQ page with a tidy and user-friendly design. It should have:

1. Heading: "Frequently Asked Questions"
2. Short intro: "Here you'll find answers to the most common questions we receive."
3. Questions organized in categories with accordion elements:

Category: "General"
- "What does [company name] do?" — [answer]
- "Where are you located?" — [answer]

Category: "Pricing and Payment"
- "How much do the services cost?" — [answer]
- "What payment methods do you accept?" — [answer]

Category: "Delivery and Warranty"
- "What is the delivery time?" — [answer]
- "Do you offer a warranty?" — [answer]

4. CTA at the bottom: "Have other questions?" with a contact button

Design: clean and organized with good spacing. Categories should have clear headings. Accordion elements with plus/minus icons and smooth animations.
```

## Tips

<Info>
  **Start simple.** You don't need to have all the content ready from the start. Create the page with what you have, and add more over time.
</Info>

<Warning>
  **Plan the structure.** Think through which pages you need before you start. A tidy menu structure makes it easy for customers to find what they're looking for.
</Warning>
