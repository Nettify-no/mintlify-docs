## When do you need a new page?

Most websites have more than just a homepage. With Nettify you can easily create new subpages to organize your content.

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Typical pages for businesses" icon="building">
    - Homepage
    - About us
    - Services / Products
    - Pricing
    - Contact
  </Card>
  <Card title="Extra pages to consider" icon="sparkles">
    - Blog / News
    - Gallery / Projects
    - FAQ
    - Careers / Open positions
    - Privacy / Terms
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

---

## How to create a new page

<Steps>
  <Step title="Tell Nettify AI what the page should be about">
    Give a clear description of what the page should contain and who it's for.

    <Tip>
      **Example:** "Create a new page called 'Our Services'. It should show our four main services: painting, carpentry, plumbing and electrical work. Each service should have a short description and an image."
    </Tip>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Nettify AI builds the page for you">
    Nettify AI creates the page with content, layout and design that matches the rest of your website.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Review and adjust">
    Go through the page. Want to change something? Just say so.

    - "Add prices to each service"
    - "Change the order so painting comes first"
    - "Add a contact button at the bottom"
  </Step>

  <Step title="The page is automatically added to the menu">
    The new page appears in your website's navigation so visitors can easily find it.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Examples of new pages

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Services page">
    "Create a page for 'Services' with three sections: Residential Sales, Commercial Property, and Rental Management. Each section should have a heading, a short text and a 'Read more' button."
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="About us page">
    "Create an 'About us' page. We're called Fjordbygg Ltd and have been in business since 2008. We have 12 employees in Stavanger and specialize in renovation of older buildings. Show the team with names and titles."
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Contact page">
    "Create a contact page with address, phone, email and a contact form. Our address is 15 Main Street, Oslo. Add a map as well."
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Pricing page">
    "Create a pricing page for our gym with three subscriptions: Basic ($29/mo), Plus ($44/mo) and Premium ($59/mo). Show what's included in each."
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="FAQ page">
    "Create a FAQ page with answers to the most common questions we get:
    - How do I place an order?
    - What is the delivery time?
    - Can I return items?
    - Do you offer a warranty?"
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Tips

<Info>
  **Start simple.** You don't need to have all the content ready from the start. Create the page with what you have, and add more over time.
</Info>

<Warning>
  **Plan the structure.** Think through which pages you need before you start. A tidy menu structure makes it easy for customers to find what they're looking for.
</Warning>
