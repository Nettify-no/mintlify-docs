## What is "Edit the website"?

Right above the chat box you'll find the **Edit the website** button. It opens a panel with ready-made actions for the most common things people do with their website — like swapping images, adding a new section or setting up a contact form.

You don't have to phrase anything yourself. Pick an action, answer a few simple questions, and Nettify AI does the rest.

<Tip>
  **Not sure where to start?** Always start here. This is the easiest way to
  edit — perfect when you know *what* you want, but not quite how to explain
  it.
</Tip>

---

## How to do it

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the panel">
    Open your website in Nettify and click **Edit the website** right above the chat box. The "What would you like to do?" panel opens.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Pick a category">
    On the left you'll see categories like **Recommended**, **Text & images**, **Sections** and **Appearance**. Click the one that fits.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Pick an action">
    Click the card that describes what you want to do — for example **Swap image** or **Add new page**. Some actions open a simple form where you fill in details, others prepare a ready-made instruction in the chat box.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Fill in and send">
    Answer the questions in the form and send. Nettify AI makes the change, and you see the result in the preview right away.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## What's in the panel?

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Text & images">
    Swap images, edit text, change the tone of your copy, add a new section, or let customers book directly.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Sections">
    Add a call-to-action button, benefits, FAQ, opening hours, "how we work", price comparison and more.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Showcase">
    Video, portfolio, image carousel, before/after images, customer testimonials, team and partner logos.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Marketing">
    Newsletter, campaign banner, map, countdown, popup, social icons and a sticky contact button.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Appearance">
    Change colors, fonts, layout, button styles and animations.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Pages">
    Add a new page — for example pricing, gallery, FAQ, booking or reviews. Also edit the menu and footer.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Blog and Google">
    Set up a blog, write new posts, and make your pages visible in Google search.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
