## What is "Select element"?

Sometimes it's easier to *point* than to explain. With **Select element** you click directly on the part of the website you want to change — a heading, an image, a button or a whole section — and write what should happen to just that part.

That way you don't have to explain where on the page the change should happen. Nettify AI knows exactly which area you mean.

<Tip>
  **Perfect when:** you want to change one specific thing, but it's hard to
  describe where it is. "That button over there" suddenly becomes very easy.
</Tip>

---

## How to do it

<Steps>
  <Step title="Click «Select element»">
    In the chat box at the bottom you'll find the **Select element** button (next to the send button). Click it.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Click what you want to change">
    The website enters selection mode. Click a heading, an image, a button, a menu or a whole section in the preview. The area you pick gets highlighted with a yellow outline.

    <Tip>
      Changed your mind? Click **Cancel selection** or press Esc.
    </Tip>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Describe the change">
    A small box appears next to the area you picked: **"What would you like to change?"** Write what should happen — for example "Make this heading shorter and more catchy". You can also attach images.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Click «Apply change»">
    Nettify AI changes only the area you marked. The rest of the website is left untouched.
  </Step>
</Steps>

---

## Examples of what you can write

- "Swap this image for something that fits a hair salon"
- "Make this button green and change the text to 'Book now'"
- "Rewrite this section so it sounds more personal"
- "Make this heading bigger"
