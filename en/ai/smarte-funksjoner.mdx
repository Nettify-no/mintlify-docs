## Make your website smarter

Nettify AI can do much more than change text and images. Here's an overview of smart features you can ask Nettify AI to create — from countdowns and price calculators to newsletters and image galleries.

---

## Engage your customers

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Newsletter signup" icon="envelope-open-text">
    Add a field where visitors can sign up for your newsletter. Perfect for staying in touch with customers and sharing offers.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Before and after gallery" icon="images">
    Show your work results with side-by-side images. Ideal for hairdressers, contractors, cleaning companies and interior designers.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Countdown / Campaign timer" icon="clock">
    Add a countdown to an offer, opening day or event. Creates urgency and engagement.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Popup with offer" icon="tag">
    Show a special offer or discount code as a popup when visitors arrive — or when they're about to leave.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

---

## Build trust

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Partner logo row" icon="handshake">
    Display logos of brands, suppliers or clients you work with. Builds credibility.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Certifications and approvals" icon="certificate">
    Show that your business is certified, approved or a member of an industry organization.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Numbers that count" icon="chart-line">
    Display key figures like "500+ happy customers", "15 years of experience" or "4.9 out of 5 stars" with animated counters.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Google reviews" icon="google" color="#4285F4">
    Embed Google reviews directly on your website so new customers can see what others think.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

---

## Practical tools

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Price calculator" icon="calculator">
    Let customers calculate prices based on their choices — for example number of hours, type of service, or project size.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Step-by-step process" icon="list-ol">
    Show customers how it works to do business with you — from first contact to final delivery.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Opening hours" icon="business-time">
    Display when your business is open with a clean and clear overview. Can also show if you're open right now.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Download button" icon="download">
    Offer downloads of menus, price lists, brochures or other documents directly from your website.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

## Example instructions

```
"Create a newsletter section with an email field and the button 'Keep me updated'.
Place it just above the footer."
```

```
"Add a section with key figures:
- 500+ projects delivered
- 15 years of experience
- 4.8 out of 5 on Google
Use animated counters."
```

```
"Create a price calculator where the customer selects the type of service and number of hours,
and gets an estimated price. Place it on the services page."
```

```
"Show a before and after slider with images of renovation projects.
Place it in a separate section on the homepage."
```
