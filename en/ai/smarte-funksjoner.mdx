## Make your website smarter

Nettify AI can do much more than change text and images. Here's an overview of smart features you can ask Nettify AI to create — from countdowns and price calculators to newsletters and image galleries.

Each feature has a ready-made prompt you can copy and paste directly into Nettify AI. Customize it with your own info and you're good to go.

---

## Engage your customers

### Newsletter signup

Want to stay in touch with your customers after they visit your website? With a newsletter signup, visitors can leave their email so you can send them offers, news and updates. A simple way to build a customer list and bring people back to your site.

<Tip>
  **Copy this prompt and paste it into Nettify AI:**
</Tip>

```
Create a newsletter section just above the footer. It should have:
- A heading: "Stay in the loop"
- A short text: "Sign up for our newsletter to get exclusive offers and news straight to your inbox."
- An email field with placeholder text "Your email address"
- A button with the text "Subscribe"
- Design: clean and simple, with a background color that stands out slightly from the rest of the page. Centered layout.

When someone submits their email, show a confirmation: "Thanks! You're now subscribed to our newsletter."
```

### Before and after gallery

Nothing sells your work better than showing real results. A before-and-after gallery lets potential customers see the difference you make, which builds trust and helps them say yes faster. Great for any business where the results speak for themselves.

<Tip>
  **Copy this prompt and paste it into Nettify AI:**
</Tip>

```
Create a "Before and After" section on the homepage. It should have:
- A heading: "See the results of our work"
- A short text: "Drag the slider to see the difference."
- An interactive image slider where you can drag between the "before" and "after" image
- Space for at least 3 projects, with a title below each (e.g. "Kitchen renovation", "Bathroom remodel", "Exterior work")
- Modern and clean design. Use images that fill the full width of the section.
```

### Countdown / Campaign timer

Running a sale, launching something new, or hosting an event? A countdown timer creates excitement and urgency so customers act now instead of later. It is one of the easiest ways to boost sales during a promotion.

<Tip>
  **Copy this prompt and paste it into Nettify AI:**
</Tip>

```
Create a campaign countdown at the top of the homepage, just below the menu. It should have:
- A background that stands out (e.g. dark or accent color)
- The text "Limited time offer!" or similar
- A countdown clock showing days, hours, minutes and seconds down to a date you choose
- A button with the text "See the offer" that scrolls to the offer section or a separate page
- Design: compact banner style, not too dominant, but visible enough to catch attention.
```

### Popup with offer

Turn more visitors into customers by showing them a special deal right when they arrive or before they leave. A popup with a discount code or offer gives people a reason to take action and can really boost your sales.

<Tip>
  **Copy this prompt and paste it into Nettify AI:**
</Tip>

```
Create a popup that appears when visitors have been on the page for 5 seconds. It should have:
- A heading: "Get 10% off your first order!"
- A short text: "Sign up for our newsletter and receive the discount code by email."
- An email field and a button "Get discount code"
- A "Close" button or X in the top right corner
- Design: modern modal with dark overlay background. Should look professional and not pushy.

After submission, show the text: "Check your inbox! The discount code is on its way."
```

### Certifications and approvals

Customers want to know they are working with someone qualified. Showing your certifications and approvals on your website helps people feel confident choosing you over competitors who do not display theirs.

<Tip>
  **Copy this prompt and paste it into Nettify AI:**
</Tip>

```
Create a section showing our certifications and approvals. It should have:
- A heading: "Certified and approved"

### Google reviews

Most customers check reviews before choosing a business. By showing your Google reviews right on your website, visitors do not have to leave to look you up. It builds instant trust and makes it easier for people to pick you.

<Tip>
  **Copy this prompt and paste it into Nettify AI:**
</Tip>

```
Create a section showing Google reviews from our customers. It should have:
- A heading: "What our customers say"

  - Stars (1-5)

## Practical tools

### Price calculator

Customers love knowing the cost upfront. A price calculator lets visitors figure out what your services will cost them, which saves you time answering the same questions and helps customers feel confident enough to reach out.

<Tip>
  **Copy this prompt and paste it into Nettify AI:**
</Tip>

```
Create a price calculator on our services page. It should have:
- A heading: "Calculate your price"
- Step 1: Choose service type (dropdown or buttons): "Consultation", "Standard package", "Premium package"
- Step 2: Choose number of hours or scope (slider or number field)
- Step 3: Any extras (checkboxes): "Weekend work (+20%)", "Emergency repair (+30%)"
- Show a calculated total price that updates in real-time
- A button "Get a quote" that opens a contact form with the selected service pre-filled
- Design: clean and interactive, with clear steps and visible price display.
```

### Step-by-step process

When people understand how working with you works, they are more likely to get in touch. A clear step-by-step process removes the guesswork and makes your business feel organized and easy to work with.

<Tip>
  **Copy this prompt and paste it into Nettify AI:**
</Tip>

```
Create a "How it works" section showing our process step by step. It should have:
- A heading: "How we work"
- 4 steps with numbered circles, icon, title and short description:
  1. "Get in touch" — "Send us an inquiry and we'll have a no-obligation chat."
  2. "Site visit" — "We come to you and assess the job that needs doing."
  3. "Quote" — "You receive a detailed quote within 2 business days."
  4. "Delivery" — "We complete the job on time and on budget."
- The steps should be connected with a line or arrows between them
- Design: horizontal layout on desktop, vertical on mobile. Clean and clear.
```

### Opening hours

Save yourself from answering "when are you open?" over and over. Showing your opening hours clearly on your website helps customers plan their visit and cuts down on unnecessary calls and messages.

<Tip>
  **Copy this prompt and paste it into Nettify AI:**
</Tip>

```
Create an opening hours section in the contact area of the homepage. It should have:
- A heading: "Opening hours"
- A neat table or list with the days:
  - Monday – Friday: 08:00 – 16:00
  - Saturday: 10:00 – 14:00
  - Sunday: Closed
- Clearly show if we are "Open now" or "Closed now" based on the current time, with a green or red indicator
- Design: compact and easy to read. Use a clock icon next to the heading.
```
