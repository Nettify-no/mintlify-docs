## Reach customers in multiple languages

Do you run a business with international customers, tourists or English-speaking staff? With Nettify AI you can easily add a language switcher to your website, so visitors can switch between languages as needed.

---

## How to add a language switcher

<Steps>
  <Step title="Ask for a language switcher">
    Tell Nettify AI that you want a button or menu where visitors can switch languages.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Translate the content">
    Nettify AI can translate your website text automatically. You can also provide your own translations if you want full control.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Place the language switcher">
    Choose where the language button should go — usually in the navigation menu at the top of the page.
  </Step>
</Steps>

---

## Example instructions

```
"Add a language switcher in the navigation with Norwegian and English.
Use flag icons for Norway and the UK.
Translate all content to English."
```

```
"Add an 'English' button at the top right of the website.
When clicked, all text on the page should display in English."
```

```
"Translate the entire homepage to English and add a
language switcher in the menu where you can switch between NO and EN."
```

## Tips for multilingual websites

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Start with the homepage" icon="house">
    Translate the most important pages first — homepage, services and contact. You can always add more pages later.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Use flags or codes" icon="flag">
    A Norwegian and a British flag, or simple codes like **NO / EN**, make it easy for visitors to find the language option.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Double-check the translation" icon="spell-check">
    Nettify AI translates well, but it's always wise to review the text — especially industry terms and names that should be kept as-is.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Remember contact info" icon="address-card">
    Addresses, phone numbers and email are the same in all languages — but opening hours and greetings should be adapted.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

<Info>
  **Need more languages?** Nettify AI supports not just Norwegian and English. You can add languages like Swedish, Danish, Polish, Arabic and many more — just let us know!
</Info>
